Pesto

Description

An ancient recipe from the romans.

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Pine nut
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  1. Grind the pine nut garlic and salt into a thick paste
  2. Mixed in the basil, and slowly drizzile in olive oil, continue to grind until smooth texture
  3. Serve with freshly cooked pasta, or dipped with crackers
