Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

An asian classic

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Soy Sauce
  1. Pre-heat a wok on high heat
  2. Crack egg into the wok and scramble. Throw in the garlic and shrimp, cook until nearly done.
  3. Toss in the rice and soy sauce, stir well.
